# Government & State Registration Guide
## How to Register Your State or Country with SHEtoken

> By registering with SHEtoken, your government's women's programs become globally visible, investable, and financially rewarded. This guide explains the full registration process.

---

## Why Register?

When your state or country registers with SHEtoken you unlock five powerful benefits:

| Benefit | What It Means |
|---|---|
| ✅ **Verified Blue Tick** | Your data is independently verified — signalling credibility to global investors |
| ✅ **Quarterly WEI Updates** | Your score updates every quarter instead of annually — faster investment signals |
| ✅ **WEI Impact Bonds** | Raise direct capital from global investors to fund women's programs |
| ✅ **Global Leaderboard Visibility** | Appear on the shetoken.org dashboard as a trackable investment destination |
| ✅ **ESG Capital Attraction** | Verified improving states attract institutional ESG funds and impact investors |

---

## Who Can Register?

- National governments
- State governments (e.g. West Bengal, Kerala, Maharashtra)
- Provincial or regional authorities
- Any government body with verifiable women's program data

---

## Step-by-Step Registration

### Step 1 — Submit an Expression of Interest

Email **contact@shetoken.org** with:

- Name of your government body / state / country
- Primary contact person and designation
- Brief description of women's programs you would like to register
- Estimated number of beneficiaries

We will respond within 5 business days with onboarding details.

---

### Step 2 — Prepare Your Program Data

You will need to provide verified data across one or more WEI pillars:

**Economic Inclusion Programs**
```
- Program name
- Start date
- Number of female beneficiaries (verified count)
- Average monthly benefit / loan amount
- Geographic coverage (districts / blocks)
- Independent audit or verification source
```

**Education Programs**
```
- Program name
- Number of girls enrolled / retained
- Dropout rate before and after program
- Age group covered
- Independent enrollment data source (e.g. DISE)
```

**Health Programs**
```
- Program name
- Number of beneficiaries
- Key health outcome metrics (e.g. maternal mortality rate, immunisation coverage)
- Data source (e.g. NFHS, WHO district data)
```

**Safety & Legal Programs**
```
- Program name
- Type of intervention (legal aid, shelter, helpline, etc.)
- Number of cases / beneficiaries served
- Outcome metrics where available
```

---

### Step 3 — Data Verification

All submitted data is verified through a two-step process:

**3a. Cross-reference check**
Our data team cross-references your submissions against:
- National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) — for India
- UNESCO / World Bank district-level data
- Independent NGO or academic reports
- State government annual reports

**3b. DAO community review**
A 15-day community review period allows token holders to raise questions or flag discrepancies via GitHub Issues labelled `gov-verification`.

---

### Step 4 — Earn Your Blue Tick

Once data is verified:

- Your state/country profile goes live on the shetoken.org dashboard
- A **Blue Tick** appears next to your name signalling verified status
- Your WEI sub-token (e.g. `$SHE-WB`) becomes active on the leaderboard
- You receive quarterly WEI score updates instead of annual

---

### Step 5 — Issue a WEI Impact Bond (Optional)

The most powerful feature of registration. A WEI Impact Bond lets you raise direct capital from global investors to fund women's programs.

**How it works:**

```
1. Government defines a program target
   (e.g. expand Lakshmi Bhandar to 3 crore beneficiaries)

2. Government issues a WEI Impact Bond
   (e.g. $SHE-WB Impact Bond Series 1 — $5M raise)

3. Global investors buy the bond
   (betting that West Bengal's WEI score will rise over 3 years)

4. Government uses capital to expand the program

5. Score rises → bond matures → investors profit
   Score falls → investors bear the risk

6. Women benefit regardless of outcome
```

**Bond parameters are set collaboratively** between the government and the SHEtoken DAO. All bond terms are published publicly on-chain.

## Data Submission Format

Submit your data as a CSV file using the following schema:

```csv
program_name, state_or_country, pillar, beneficiary_count,
start_year, data_source, verification_body, notes
```

**Example:**
```csv
Lakshmi Bhandar, West Bengal, Economic Inclusion, 24100000,
2021, State Government Portal, NABARD audit, Monthly cash transfer ₹1500-1700
```

A full schema reference is available at: `data/schema.json`

---

## Quarterly Reporting Requirements

Once registered, governments must submit quarterly data updates to maintain Blue Tick status:

| Quarter | Submission Deadline | Data Required |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 (Jan–Mar) | 30 April | Updated beneficiary counts, new program launches |
| Q2 (Apr–Jun) | 31 July | Mid-year enrollment/outcome data |
| Q3 (Jul–Sep) | 31 October | Crime statistics update (NCRB quarterly release) |
| Q4 (Oct–Dec) | 31 January | Annual summary, audit report |

Failure to submit quarterly data results in reversion to annual update cycle. Blue Tick status is maintained as long as data quality standards are met.

---

## Privacy & Data Standards

- No individual-level data is collected or published
- Only aggregated program statistics are used
- All submitted data is stored on IPFS — immutable and tamper-proof
- Governments retain full ownership of their program data
- Data can be withdrawn at any time (historical scores remain on record)
