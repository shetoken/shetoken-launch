# How to Buy & Invest in SHEtoken ($SHE)
### A Plain English Guide for New Investors

> No crypto experience needed. If you have a smartphone and $10, you can invest in SHEtoken.

---

## What Are You Actually Buying?

When you buy $SHE tokens you are buying a financial stake in the world getting better for women.

SHEtoken is powered by the **Women's Empowerment Index (WEI)** — a real data score that measures women's safety, education, economic inclusion, and health across every country. When that score improves globally, your tokens become more valuable. When it falls, tokens are burned — making the ones you hold rarer.

This is not speculation. This is accountability finance.

## Step-by-Step: How to Buy

### Step 1 — Get a Crypto Wallet

A crypto wallet is your personal bank account for digital currency. Nobody controls it but you — no bank, no middleman.

**Download MetaMask** — it's free, trusted, and takes 5 minutes to set up.

- iOS: Search "MetaMask" in the App Store
- Android: Search "MetaMask" in Google Play
- Desktop: Visit [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)

> **Important:** When MetaMask gives you a 12-word "Secret Recovery Phrase" — write it down on paper and store it somewhere safe. This is the only way to recover your wallet. Never share it with anyone.

---

### Step 2 — Buy Ethereum (ETH)

SHEtoken runs on the Ethereum blockchain, so you first need to buy Ethereum (ETH) — the currency used to pay for transactions.

**Where to buy ETH:**

| Exchange | Best For | Region |
|---|---|---|
| [Coinbase](https://coinbase.com) | Beginners, clean interface | Global |
| [Binance](https://binance.com) | Lower fees, more options | Global |
| [WazirX](https://wazirx.com) | Indian rupee deposits | India |
| [CoinDCX](https://coindcx.com) | Indian investors | India |

**Minimum to start:** You can begin with as little as $10 / ₹800.

**How to buy:**
1. Create an account on any exchange above
2. Complete identity verification (KYC — takes 5–10 minutes)
3. Deposit money via bank transfer, UPI, or card
4. Buy ETH — the amount you want to invest

---

### Step 3 — Transfer ETH to Your MetaMask Wallet

1. Open MetaMask and copy your wallet address (starts with `0x...`)
2. In your exchange, go to **Withdraw** or **Send**
3. Select ETH and paste your MetaMask address
4. Confirm the transfer

Wait 2–5 minutes for it to arrive in MetaMask.

---

### Step 4 — Swap ETH for $SHE on Uniswap

Uniswap is a decentralised exchange — like a currency counter — where you swap one crypto for another directly.

1. Go to [uniswap.org](https://uniswap.org)
2. Click **Launch App**
3. Connect your MetaMask wallet
4. In the **From** field — select ETH
5. In the **To** field — search for **SHE** or paste the SHEtoken contract address
6. Enter the amount you want to swap
7. Click **Swap** and confirm in MetaMask

> **Gas fees:** Ethereum transactions have small fees (called "gas"). Always keep a little extra ETH in your wallet to cover these — typically $1–5 per transaction.

---

### Step 5 — Your Tokens Are Yours

Your $SHE tokens now appear in your MetaMask wallet. You own them completely — no exchange, no company, no intermediary can touch them.

Track your holdings and the live WEI score at **[shetoken.org](https://shetoken.org)**

## Three Ways to Make Money

### 1. Price Appreciation

Buy $SHE now. As the global WEI score improves — more girls stay in school, crimes against women fall, women's wages rise — demand for the token increases and so does the price. Sell higher than you bought.

> **Example:** You buy 1,000 $SHE at ₹8 each (₹8,000 invested). Two years later, India's WEI score rises as Kanyashree enrols record numbers of girls and JEEViKA lifts 50,000 women out of poverty. Price rises to ₹22. You sell 500 tokens = ₹11,000 back. You've made ₹3,000 profit and still hold 500 tokens.

### 2. Token Scarcity via Burns

When the WEI score falls — a crime spike, a drop in female literacy, a rise in poverty — tokens are **permanently burned** from the supply. Less supply + same or growing demand = each token you hold becomes rarer and more valuable.

This means even bad news in the world can increase the value of tokens already held — which creates a financial incentive for investors to care about crime reduction.

### 3. Staking Rewards

Lock up your $SHE tokens for 6–12 months and earn bonus tokens as a reward — similar to earning interest in a savings account.

- 6-month stake: earn additional tokens proportional to your holding
- 12-month stake: higher reward rate
- Staking strengthens price stability and rewards long-term believers over short-term traders

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to know about crypto to invest?**
No. Follow the 5 steps above. MetaMask and Uniswap are both designed for non-technical users.

**What is the minimum investment?**
You can start with as little as $10 / ₹800 worth of ETH.

**Is my investment safe?**
Your tokens are stored in your own MetaMask wallet — nobody can access them without your secret recovery phrase. However, all crypto investments carry risk. Only invest what you can afford to lose.

**How do I know the WEI score is accurate?**
All WEI calculations are based on publicly available data from UN Women, World Bank, UNESCO, and UNODC. The full methodology is published at [github.com/shetoken](https://github.com/shetoken). Anyone can audit, challenge, or replicate every score.

**When can I sell?**
Anytime. Once you hold $SHE in your MetaMask wallet, you can swap back to ETH on Uniswap at any time.

**What is the WEI Impact Fund?**
25% of all SHEtoken supply is held in a transparent DAO-governed fund. This fund distributes grants to NGOs working in women's literacy, legal aid, anti-violence, and economic empowerment programs. When you hold $SHE, you're also funding real-world change.
