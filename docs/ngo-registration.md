# NGO & Program Registration Guide
## How to Register Your Program with SHEtoken

> Your work already changes women's lives. SHEtoken makes it globally visible, financially measurable, and directly investable. Registration is free.

---

## Why Register Your NGO?

| Benefit | What It Means |
|---|---|
| ✅ **Program ID on Dashboard** | Your program appears on shetoken.org — visible to global investors and donors |
| ✅ **Direct Token Allocation** | Investors can allocate $SHE tokens directly to your program |
| ✅ **Milestone-Based Funding** | Tokens are released as your program hits verified milestones |
| ✅ **WEI Pillar Impact** | Your program data feeds directly into the WEI index — moving the score |
| ✅ **Global Credibility** | Independent verification of your impact metrics |

---

## Who Can Register?

Any NGO, social enterprise, or community program that:

- Works directly on women's empowerment, education, safety, health, or economic inclusion
- Can provide verifiable impact data
- Has been operating for at least 1 year
- Is legally registered in its country of operation

---

## Registration Steps

### Step 1 — Submit Your Profile

Email **contact@shetoken.org** with:

```
Organisation name:
Country / State:
Year founded:
Registration number (legal entity):
Primary focus area: (Education / Economic / Health / Safety / Empowerment)
Brief description of your program (100 words):
Website / social media:
Contact person name and email:
```

---

### Step 2 — Define Your Milestones

Milestones are the verified checkpoints that trigger token releases to your program. You define them — we verify them.

**Examples of strong milestones:**

| Program Type | Example Milestone |
|---|---|
| Microfinance / SHG | 5,000 loans disbursed to women |
| Education | 1,000 girls enrolled and retained for full academic year |
| Legal Aid | 500 cases of gender-based violence resolved |
| Health | 10,000 women receiving maternal health checkups |
| Economic | 2,000 women-owned businesses registered |
| Anti-trafficking | 200 survivors provided shelter and rehabilitation |

**Milestones must be:**
- Specific and measurable
- Independently verifiable (third party or government data)
- Time-bound (quarterly or annual)
- Directly related to women's empowerment outcomes

---

### Step 3 — Verification

Your submitted data is verified against:

- Government records (NCRB, DISE, NSSO for India)
- International databases (UN Women, World Bank, UNESCO)
- Independent auditor reports
- Academic research where available

A 15-day community review period follows. Token holders can raise questions via GitHub Issues labelled `ngo-verification`.

---

### Step 4 — Go Live on the Dashboard

Once verified:

- Your program appears on the shetoken.org NGO directory
- Investors and donors can allocate $SHE tokens directly to your program
- Each milestone hit triggers an automatic token release from the WEI Impact Fund
- Your program data feeds into your state's WEI pillar score

---

## How Token Allocation Works

```
Investor holds 10,000 $SHE tokens
↓
Investor allocates 1,000 tokens to JEEViKA (Bihar)
↓
JEEViKA hits milestone: 50,000 new SHG members
↓
Milestone verified on-chain via oracle
↓
1,000 tokens released to JEEViKA's registered wallet
↓
JEEViKA converts tokens to fiat via Uniswap
↓
Funds used for program expansion
```

All token flows are publicly visible on-chain. No intermediaries. No fees deducted.

## Data You Will Need to Submit

### For Economic Programs
```
- Number of women members / beneficiaries
- Total loans / grants disbursed (₹ or $)
- Average income change per beneficiary (if tracked)
- Number of women-owned businesses created
- Source: Bank statements, NABARD audit, MFI data
```

### For Education Programs
```
- Number of girls enrolled
- Dropout rate before and after intervention
- Literacy rate improvement (if tracked)
- Geographic coverage
- Source: DISE enrollment data, school records
```

### For Health Programs
```
- Number of beneficiaries receiving care
- Key health metrics (maternal mortality, vaccination rates)
- Healthcare access improvement data
- Source: NFHS data, district health reports
```

### For Safety / Legal Programs
```
- Number of cases handled
- Number of survivors supported
- Legal outcomes (cases filed, convictions, restraining orders)
- Source: Court records, police FIR data, internal case management
```

## Reporting Requirements

Registered NGOs submit data **quarterly:**

| Quarter | Due Date | What to Submit |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | 30 April | Beneficiary count update, new milestone progress |
| Q2 | 31 July | Mid-year outcome data |
| Q3 | 31 October | Annual milestone assessment |
| Q4 | 31 January | Full year impact report |

Reports are submitted via the secure API or email to contact@shetoken.org.

Failure to report for two consecutive quarters results in program suspension from the dashboard until reporting resumes.

---

## What SHEtoken Does NOT Do

- We do not take a percentage of your funds
- We do not control how you spend token allocations
- We do not require exclusivity — register with other impact platforms freely
- We do not publish individual beneficiary data — only aggregate statistics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is registration free?**
Yes. Registration, verification, and dashboard listing are all free.

**Do we need to hold crypto to participate?**
No. You receive $SHE tokens which can be converted to fiat currency via Uniswap. We can assist with this process.

**What if our data is not digitised?**
We can work with paper records, manual counts, and government-issued certificates. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

**Can a program in any country register?**
Yes. We welcome programs from any country. India programs receive additional visibility due to our strong state-level data infrastructure.

**How long does verification take?**
Typically 3–4 weeks from submission to dashboard listing.
